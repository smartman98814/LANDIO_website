Property Description

Seize the chance to own this rare 144 Acre turnkey farm in Central Colorado with a modern warehouse and state-of-the-art greenhouse, just 4 miles north of Moffat off HWY 17. This live/work property offers breathtaking views, privacy, and quick access to Saguache, Alamosa, and Salida.

Whether you're looking to start a commercial farm, invest in a remote-operated venture, or live a self-sufficient lifestyle on a beautiful Colorado homestead, this fully automated farm is your gateway to endless possibilities.

Historically a working cattle ranch and later a flourishing farm, the previous owners have invested heavily in infrastructure, ensuring you get the best value for your investment. ‍

‍

Access, Electricity, & More

There are 0.5 miles of frontage road along the eastern boundary of State Highway 17, 0.4 miles along the northern boundary of County Road X, and 0.5 miles along the western boundary of County Road 59.

With a 1200-amp service panel, there is enough power from the overhead lines running along Highway 17 and County Road 59 to power commercial operations.

Low Taxes & Easy Construction

Take advantage of low property taxes, and benefit from a county with minimal construction restrictions and inspections so you can do your dream your way.‍

‍

Home Sweet Mountain Home

The property has a fully renovated (2019) 1999 double-wide manufactured home with 5 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms. It offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Sangre de Cristo and La Garita Mountain ranges and occasional elk, deer, and antelope visits.‍

‍

Efficient Irrigation & Water Rights

Manage irrigation and security remotely via computer or phone app. The farm includes a remote-controlled Zimmatic Center Pivot that irrigates 63.5 acres—yours with the purchase!

‍Year-Round Water Flow

Essential for farming in Colorado, this property comes with invaluable water rights and 4 adjudicated artesian water wells, making it an exceptional asset. One produces 450 GPM, two at 30 GPM, and one at 50 GPM.

Additionally, there are two holding ponds on the property's NW and SW corners. These enhancements allow for efficient water use, even under strict water regulations.

As an added perk, these artesian wells never freeze all the way through due to their constant flow, which is perfect for stocking ponds with trout.‍

‍

Advanced Greenhouse

Built in 2019, the 4-bay, 11,800 sq ft greenhouse is a marvel. It features a concrete elevation foundation, sidewalks, loading bays, galvanized steel walls, and a polycarbonate roof. The entire structure is spray-foamed for insulation, perfect for year-round growing.

Ideal for Organic Growers

The advanced air filtration system prevents airborne contaminants and allows you to grow heirloom tomatoes, pineapples, artichokes, and microgreens in the equipped greenhouses.

Inside, you'll find:

•4 HVAC units for heating
•3 large wet wall units for cooling
•Adequate venting and lighting for optimal growing conditions

Everything you need is already in place:

•Rolling benches
•Gavita Pro-E-Series lighting
•Fully automated light deprivation
•Intake shutters and exhaust fans
•Hunter Irrigation Controller
•Dehumidifiers
•Trident Nutrient System
•RO/UV light water filtration system
•3-phase power from overhead power lines

All equipment is just 2 years old and in perfect working order.‍

‍

Warehouse Highlights 

The 3,800 sq ft heated and insulated warehouse sits on a concrete slab and includes an office and bathroom. A 7' metal chain link fence with barbwire and ADT video surveillance secures the entire site.‍

‍

Looking for a Little Adventure?

Colorado boasts countless natural wonders, and several are well under 2 hours from the farm:

Explore the serene Baca National Wildlife Refuge just minutes from the property, and discover the vast Rio Grande National Forest, teeming with diverse wildlife and stunning landscapes. The San Luis State Wildlife Area offers unique habitats for birdwatching and fishing, while the iconic Great Sand Dunes National Park promises adventure with its towering dunes and scenic trails.

Don’t miss the charming mountain town of South Fork, CO, a great base camp for exploring the breathtaking Collegiate Peaks, known for their soaring summits and epic 14-ers. Finally, unwind at the tranquil Terrace Reservoir, an ideal spot for fishing and soaking up Colorado's unparalleled beauty.‍

‍

This Cutting-Edge Farm in Colorado Won’t Last Long

Whether you're an investor looking for a well-equipped farm you can operate remotely or seeking a thriving homestead with modern amenities, this property checks all the boxes. Grow your crops, raise livestock, and run a business all in one place—with water rights!—an exceptional find in Colorado.

Property Description

20 Wooded Mountain Acres bordering nearly 300,000 Acres of the Cibola National Forest less than 1 mile from "The Falls" waterfall.

This parcel borders the Cibola National Forest along the West boundary and is covered with dense mature Ponderosa Pines.

There is seasonal water flow running through the entire Eastern portion of the property.

This beautiful forest property offers varying terrain, with the peak elevation on the Western portion near the Cibola National Forest reaching 7,640 feet. This is among one of the highest points in the area, comparable with the 7,700 foot Mesa located 4 miles South.

Bordering the Cibola National Forest comes with opportunity for extended recreation right from the property. The seasonal water flow also offers a source of water for the wildlife residing in the forest. See photos for the many wildlife tracks & droppings found on the property. 

Located in Game Management Unit 10, which is said to be desirable for Mule Deer & Elk with some of largest Bulls in New Mexico. (Download the GMU 10 Map)

For more information, visit the New Mexico Game & Fish Department Website.

The richly diverse volcanic landscape of El Malpais National Park offers solitude, recreation, and discovery. Explore cinder cones, lava tube caves, sandstone bluffs, caves and hiking trails. Wildlife abounds in the open grasslands and forests.

The property is 8.7 miles to State Highway 53 which will take you directly to San Rafael to the East, then connects to I-40 to take you into Grants and Milan.

The city of Gallup is located 52 miles from the property. There is a Walmart Supercenter, various parks, professional services, medical centers, groceries, and supplies located here. The population of Gallup, New Mexico is about 21,700.

Grants, New Mexico is also located only 61 miles East, which has a population of around 10,000 people. In Grants you will find groceries, supplies, the Cibola General Hospital, an airport and Walmart Supercenter.

Utility Lines are in the area and on nearby developed properties where land owners have homes, mobile homes, cabins, accessory buildings and corrals (see photos & satellite images).

No HOA, HOA Fees, or time limit to build.
